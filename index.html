Top Tourist Destinations

Explore some of the most remarkable places around the world.

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is an iconic symbol of France. Completed in 1889 for the World Expo, it stands in Paris and offers stunning views of the city.

Location: Paris, France

Great Wall of China

The Great Wall stretches over 13,000 miles and was built to protect Chinese states from invasions. It's one of the most impressive feats of ancient engineering.

Location: Northern China

Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu is a 15th-century Inca citadel located in the Andes Mountains. It's known for its archaeological significance and breathtaking views.

Location: Cusco Region, Peru

Sydney Opera House

The Sydney Opera House is a renowned performing arts center known for its unique sail-like design. It's a major landmark in Sydney, Australia.

Location: Sydney, Australia

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a stunning white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It's a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Location: Agra, India
